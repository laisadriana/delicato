A Better CTA

No please, do not use "click here" ever again!
It is not just awkward to use "click here" but also let's take in consideration that portable device users do not ever click anything! They tap! And there are more people tapping than clicking nowadays. So let's get over the "click here" once and for all and try these suggestions instead:

  • Get It!, I Want This!, Get My...

  • Give Me More, Show Me More, Tell Me More, Find Out More

  • Explore, Discover..., Enjoy...

  • See The Advantages, Explore The Benefits, Why...?

And if it is an official website, it is no excuse, just use the old and good "More info" and nobody will be hurt.
